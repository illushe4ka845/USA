About the Event

Green Neighbourhood Saturday is a local community event focused on sustainability, eco-awareness, and building stronger neighbourhood connections. Come together to celebrate green living and take part in eco-friendly activities for all ages!

Activities

  • Community clean-up
  • Tree planting and gardening
  • Clothing swap & recycling station
  • Kids' crafts and eco-games
  • Local farmers' market
  • Workshops on sustainable living

Location

Main Park, Greenfield Neighbourhood
123 Green Street, YourCity
